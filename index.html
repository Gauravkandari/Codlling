Some FAQs

Coaching can be beneficial for anyone who wants to improve their skills, achieve their goals, or overcome obstacles. If you are motivated to make positive changes in your life and are open to feedback and guidance, coaching may be a good fit for you.

The number of coaching sessions you need will depend on your goals and the complexity of the issues you want to address. Some clients find that just a few sessions are enough to achieve their goals, while others may benefit from ongoing coaching support over a longer period of time.

Coaching sessions can vary in length depending on the coach and the program. Typically, a coaching session lasts between 30 minutes and 1 hour.
